Ads for the 49th Super Bowl explore familiar themes: cute girls, humor and fuzzy animals.
Last year's ad featuring an unlikely friendship between one of the Budweiser Clydesdales and a cute puppy won many hearts. In this year's ad, the puppy has gone missing. Anheuser-Busch (AHBIF) says, "The Budweiser Clydesdales will tell an emotional story and help a puppy who has lost his way learn the true meaning of friendship." And for many, the true meaning of friendship is beer. Doggone!
