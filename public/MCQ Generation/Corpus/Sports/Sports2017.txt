Updated 1106 GMT (1806 HKT) March 30, 2015
Around 120,000 people packed the stands at the annual Hong Kong Rugby Sevens, a three-day tournament where 28 rugby teams go head to head for the champion title. One of the biggest events on the Hong Kong sporting calendar, spectators are also known to party hard in eye-catching costumes off-pitch.
