How to use WordNews

Overview

This is a chrome extension to help users learn vocabulary from news website.

You can choose to enable/disable the chrome extension in the settings popup and configure which websites the extension will work on. You can disable the extension entirely from the chrome://extensions url (type chrome://extensions into Chrome's address bar)

The words you learnt are remembered, as well as which words you forgot, and you will be given quizzes smartly chosen with different levels of tests based on your learning behaviour.

Using the extension

When on an article on a news website where the extension was configured to be enabled on, words in the article will be translated and replaced with its Chinese equivalent. Clicking on the Chinese translation will reveal information about the word, such as the Mandarin pronunciation of the word, and a link to dictionary entry on the word.

When the same word has been encountered and viewed multiple times, an MCQ question will be shown the next time you click on the word.
